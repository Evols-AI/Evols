## Quarterly Review Prompts

Use these during quarterly decision review:

1. **Pattern scan:** What categories am I deciding in most? Am I over-indexing on tactical decisions vs. strategic ones?
2. **Outcome review:** For decisions older than 30 days, what actually happened? Update the Actual Outcome field.
3. **Lessons:** What would I do differently? What worked better than expected?
4. **Narrative extraction:** Which decisions best demonstrate my judgment and impact? Move these to the Narrative Inventory in work-context.md.
5. **Delegation check:** Which decisions could someone else have made? Am I holding onto decisions I should push down?
